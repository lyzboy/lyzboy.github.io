Dices D&D Club

Dices is your gateway to a world of adventure, where epic tales and daring quests await. As passionate gamers, we've united to form a tight-knit community of adventurers, storytellers, and creative minds.

Sign Up

Atmosphere

Our club is a place where you can meet new people, make friends, and have fun. We are a community of people who love to play Dungeons & Dragons and other tabletop role-playing games. We welcome all players, from beginners to veterans, and we are always happy to help new players learn the game.

Game Nights

Game nights are held every Tuesday at 6:00 PM and Saturday at 12:00 PM. We play a variety of games, including Dungeons & Dragons, Pathfinder, and more. We also have a variety of board games available to play. If you're interested in joining us, please contact us for more information.

Dungeon Masters

We are always looking for new Dungeon Masters to join our club. If you are interested in becoming a Dungeon Master, please contact us for more information.

Location

We are located at 123 Main Street, Anytown, USA. Our club is open to the public, so feel free to stop by and check us out.
